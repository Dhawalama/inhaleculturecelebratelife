What we do

Three things,
done with care.

ICCL exists to advance culture in Australia. We do that in three ways — and we keep our focus narrow on purpose, so each program lands with the depth it deserves.

01 ·

Festivals & public events

We curate, produce and present free public festivals — bringing food, music and performance to parks and public spaces where everyone is welcome.

02 ·

Multicultural community

Programs that connect diverse Australian communities through shared meals, shared stages and the everyday rituals of cultural participation.

03 ·

Arts, food & heritage

Supporting artists, cooks and cultural practitioners — through performances, workshops, residencies and partnerships across Australia and overseas.

Our principal object

To advance culture in Australia by curating, producing and presenting festivals, events and programs with a focus on arts, culture and heritage representing multicultural Australia — while fostering intercultural understanding and social cohesion.
— Constitution of Inhale Culture Celebrate Life Ltd · Adopted 27 August 2025
