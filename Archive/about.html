About
ICCL.

Inhale Culture Celebrate Life Ltd — a registered Australian charity and public company limited by guarantee, established in September 2025 and based in Thornbury, Victoria.

Our purpose

Advancing
culture in multicultural Australia.

ICCL exists to advance culture in Australia — through festivals, events and programs that reflect the country's multicultural reality and foster intercultural understanding, community connection and social cohesion.

We are a small, volunteer-led charity that focuses narrowly on doing a few things well. Our flagship is MOMO Fest Melbourne. Beyond it, we partner with artists, food producers, councils and communities to bring public cultural programming to life.

"To advance culture in Australia by curating, producing and presenting festivals, events and programs with a focus on arts, culture and heritage representing multicultural Australia — while fostering intercultural understanding and social cohesion."
— Principal object · Constitution adopted 27 August 2025

Our scope

What we're
set up to do.

Our constitution authorises a broad range of cultural, educational, artistic and community activities. These define our capacity — not all are active yet. As a new organisation with one confirmed festival, we'll grow into the rest deliberately.

i.

Cultural programming

Festivals, performances, exhibitions, screenings, talks, culinary and craft programs, flagship and touring events.

ii.

Artist support

Residencies, mentorships, workshops, awards and support for artists and cultural practitioners where funds permit.

iii.

Education & engagement

Audience, participant and volunteer programs; school and university engagement; accessibility initiatives.

iv.

Documentation & preservation

Digital content, archives, recordings and documentation preserving cultural works and heritage.

v.

Partnerships

Collaboration with governments, councils, First Nations peoples, cultural bodies, education institutions, charities and international networks.

vi.

Cultural tourism

Cultural tourism activities, touring programs and collaboration with organisations in Australia and overseas with similar purposes.

Leadership

Board of
Directors.

ICCL is governed by a voluntary board of directors. Directors must be members of ICCL and serve without remuneration. The board currently has three members — the minimum required under our constitution.
